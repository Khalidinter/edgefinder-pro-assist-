## Scope

Phase 1 drops the 10 anon-role-specific write policies (INSERT/UPDATE/DELETE
on {anon}) to reduce the public schema's anon write surface. Phase 2 (not
in this migration) will drop the 11 TO-PUBLIC-ALL policies which are the
actual RLS exposure — Phase 1 alone fully closes only am_paper_trades and
pipeline_runs; the other 6 affected tables remain writable by anon via
their surviving {public} ALL policies until Phase 2 lands.

  FULLY CLOSED (anon cannot write at all):
    - am_paper_trades   (no surviving write policy)
    - pipeline_runs     (no surviving write policy)
  PARTIALLY CLOSED (anon-specific policy removed, TO PUBLIC ALL still allows):
    - am_backtest_results
    - am_backtest_runs
    - am_game_logs
    - am_player_tracking

High-value attack surface (paper-trade hit-value tampering from browser
devtools) is closed by Phase 1. Remaining {public} ALL policies close in
Phase 2.
